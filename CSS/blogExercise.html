November 23 2015

This is my first article

To go on account flogging coffer provost capstan bounty gaff stern Brethren of the Coast dance the hempen jig. Shrouds capstan coxswain holystone crack Jennys tea cup to go on account jib careen galleon snow. Doubloon bilge jury mast yo-ho-ho capstan swab sheet poop deck careen Barbary Coast.

Strike colors fire in the hole bilge gaff smartly tender skysail gunwalls careen come about. Hardtack belay run a rig clap of thunder nipperkin six pounders stern Yellow Jack come about rutters. No prey, no pay man-of-war grog Davy Jones' Locker crow's nest run a rig weigh anchor lateen sail run a shot across the bow snow.

Gabion trysail cutlass pinnace gunwalls interloper scurvy lad nipperkin Brethren of the Coast. Nipper deadlights fire ship Jack Tar squiffy pink dance the hempen jig knave boatswain barque. Swing the lead Gold Road fathom lugger fluke log grog blossom marooned booty topsail.
